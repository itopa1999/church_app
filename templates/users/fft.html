    Sound doctrine is God's roadmap for the Christian life and the life of the Church.-Bobby Jamieson

    Zeal without doctrine is like a sword in the hand of a lunatic.-John Calvin

    Those who teach by their doctrine must teach by their lives, or else they pull down with one hand what they build up with the other.-Matthew Henry

    If we go astray in our doctrine, eventually our lives will go astray as well. You cannot separate what a man believes from what he is. For this reason, doctrine is vitally important.-Martyn Lloyd-Jones

    When a man believes wrongly, he will soon act wrongly. -Charles Spurgeon

    He who is not zealous against error is not likely to be zealous for truth.-J.C.Ryle

    Doctrine is only the drawing of the bow; application is hitting the mark.- Thomas Manton

    Doctrine is the framework of life - the skeleton of truth - to be clothed and rounded out by the living grace of a holy life.-Adoniran Gordon

    No doctrine of the Christian religion is worth presenting that cannot be verified in daily life.-John Watson

    It is an undeniable truth that every doctrine that comes from God and that which does not tend to promote holiness is not of God.-George Whitefield.

    Good conduct arises out of good doctrine.-John Stott

    You can have all of your doctrines right and still not have the presence of God.-Leonard Ravenhill

    If you want to change your life for the better, you must start immediately and do it flamboyantly.-William James
