CAC YOUTH FELLOWSHIP VISION, MISSION AND VAULES

OUR VISION: (What we are called to do)
Christ-centred Youths for fulfilment in life and ministry
OUR MISSION:(How to justify our existence and make our calling sure)
Raising Christ-centred and excellent youth for all round fulfilment through teaching, discipleship, innovative evangelism and spiritual worship.

Our values and priorities are expressed in PRAYER. Effective Prayer Life remains our core value.
